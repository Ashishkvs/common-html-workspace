How to build an online store

Follow these simple steps to create an online store:

1. Join Zyro

It’s free to use the website builder and publish a site on Zyro, so play around with your preferences before committing to an eCommerce plan.

2. Pick an eCommerce template

Find a style that suits your brand and business, then make use of the drag-and-drop editor to customize it to your liking.

3. Add products to your store

Fill out your store’s shopping pages, then set your preferences (prices, promotions, payment options, and shipping)

4. Publish your store

All you need to do is click the ‘publish’ button.

5. Get marketing

Use Zyro’s powerful marketing tools to show off your business and maximize those sales right away.

Online store FAQs

Answers To Frequently Asked Online Store Questions

What is an eCommerce website?

eCommerce refers to selling and buying products or services online. An eCommerce website is where all the activity happens – it’s an online store. You can have an eCommerce website that sells to individuals, or your business could sell to other businesses. There are online stores for just about anything.

How much does it cost to start an online store?

While some platforms can cost thousands of dollars in setup fees, you don’t need to max out your credit card here. Zyro charges as little as $8.99 per month. You can often find yourself spending way more than you budgeted on launching an eCommerce platform. Not with Zyro. For the cost of a few oat milk lattes, you can start and run an online business with ease.

How long does it take to create an online store?

With platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, and Magento, it can take anything from a few days to a couple of weeks to get your store up and running, depending on your level of experience. With Zyro’s simple store builder features, however, even people with no design or coding experience can get ready to sell online in a matter of minutes.

How can I sell products from my online store?

Think about what you find useful while online shopping. Photos, prices, descriptions – add it all into your store, and you’re good to go. Use Zyro’s dashboard to track inventory, and offer a range of payment options, from credit cards to PayPal and beyond. We want you to avoid the heartbreak of a customer abandoning their shopping cart at checkout.

What do I do about shipping products to customers?

Zyro can calculate the best shipping rates for your shoppers, and help find the best-priced handlers based on your preferences. The entire delivery process can be tracked on your dashboard, so all you need to do is get on with business.

Does Zyro charge transaction fees?

No, the profit you make from your online store is all yours. Unlike some other platforms, Zyro takes zero commission and doesn’t charge transaction fees.
